What We Do

Our Services

Comprehensive environmental haulage and waste management services — delivered with Euro VI compliant vehicles and full carbon reporting across the South East.

Six Service Streams

Select a service to learn more

Increased control over transportation and haulage enables us to improve site logistics and better manage our carbon footprint — for clients and Group companies alike.

01

Haulage & Muck Away

Hazardous & inert waste removal across the South East

Kingscote Haulage removes waste materials from site. This includes moving soils and materials classified as hazardous, which may harm the environment or human health and require an extra duty of care. Clean inert waste, as well as non-hazardous materials that may have low levels of contamination, are also efficiently removed. The team carry out necessary testing to classify materials according to each site's requirements and can subsequently record and validate the removal of contaminated materials.

02

Recycled Aggregates

Crushing & screening to specification — 6F1, 6F5, Type 1–3

The team provide crushing and screening services to produce recycled aggregates for future use, grading them to specifications — such as 6F1, 6F5 and Types 1 to 3 — suitable for individual site requirements. This helps reduce the amount of waste being disposed of in landfills, cutting both costs and environmental impact.

Request a Quote →
03

Grab Hire

Day work & haulage for accessible and difficult-to-reach areas

Grab lorries can be hired for day work, muck away and haulage, providing an efficient and cost-effective waste removal solution for both accessible and inaccessible areas. Each of our trucks is fitted with up-to-date technology for the highest levels of safety, and operated by experienced drivers familiar with complex construction site environments.

Request a Quote →
04

Waste Disposal

Skips & roll-on/roll-off containers from 6 to 40 cubic yards

A variety of skips and roll-on, roll-off containers — which come in larger sizes for bigger waste volumes — can be supplied to sites to assist with waste removal. Container sizes range from six to 40 cubic yards, providing a flexible solution for projects of any scale and duration.

Request a Quote →
05

Landfill & Land Restoration

Farm restoration, noise bunds & quarry reinstatement

Additional services including farm land restoration, construction of noise bunds, and the reinstatement and restoration of old quarries and landfill sites across the South East are all catered for. EA permits, exemptions and planning permissions can all be efficiently obtained by the team, ensuring compliance from the outset.

Request a Quote →
06

Logistics & Fleet Control

Embedded logistics management — movements, costs & carbon reporting

Integrated logistics management embedded within your site team. We schedule load movements, reduce vehicle idle time, manage subcontractor hauliers and provide itemised carbon and cost reports across every movement — giving you full visibility and control of your haulage programme without the overhead of managing it internally.

How We Work

The Process

From first contact to project completion — a straightforward, transparent process built around your programme.

01

Initial Enquiry

Contact us with your project details — location, material types, estimated volumes and timescales. We'll confirm availability and arrange a site visit.

02

Site Assessment

We assess access, material classification and logistical requirements. Where required, we carry out soil testing and prepare documentation for regulatory compliance.

03

Service Delivery

Our Euro VI Scania fleet is mobilised to programme. Fleet movements are tracked in real time, with dedicated logistics management throughout the project.

04

Reporting

On completion we provide full carbon and cost reports for every movement, along with any required waste transfer documentation and regulatory records.
